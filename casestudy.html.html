Pal App Case Study

Busy pet owners often face frustrations when keeping track of their pet’s wellness. They need a solution that can help them get more organized with managing their pet’s documents and appointments. Our goal was to improve the pet’s caretaking experience so that busy pet owners can relax and have an enjoyable time and deeper connection with their pets and family.

Project Overview

The Problem:

We discovered that pet owners get stressed when they forget their pet's wellness information. Oftentimes, the pet's medical records get lost and the communication with other caretakes become ineffecient. This leads to an overlap of grooming and medical appointments, inconsistent feeding schedules, and overall stress in the caring of the pet's well-being.

The Solution:

The solution was to create a pet mobile app that not only keeps track of medical appointments, but also has the ability of inviting another caretaker so everyone is up to date with feeding schedules, medical appointments/records, etc.

Phase 1: User Research

During our user research phase, our goal was to learn how people currently keep track of their pet's wellness. In order to find out more details on this, we conducted 5 remote interviews and 1 survey that resulted in 29 responses. After conducting the interviews and survey, we analazyed the data by creating an affinity diagram which later helped us identify our user persona, Gabby Smith.

Phase 2: Definition and Synthesis

After knowing what challenges and goals Gabby faced, we came up with our user Insight and problem statement which helped us develop a better understanding of our user and to validate some of our ideas.

User Insight

Gabby, a young pet lover, gets stressed when forgetting about her pet’s wellness information. Her husband offers to help her but he does not know what services need to be booked since the records are disorganized. Gabby worries that they may overlap the appointments, feeding and medication schedules which could cause a problem in their pet’s health. Gabby needs a better way to communicate with her husband and store their pet’s records. This will ensure that their pet stays healthy and she can be more relaxed and have a more enjoyable time with her family and pets.

Problem Statement

During interviews, we discovered that pet owners get stressed when forgetting important pet’s wellness information. Oftentimes the pet’s medical records get lost and the communication with other pet caretakers is inefficient, which leads to forgetting medical and grooming appointments, inconsistent feeding and medication schedules, frustration and misunderstandings. We observed pet owners need a better way to communicate and share pet’s care information, keep track of appointments and store pet’s records. We observed that without an alternative, pet owners rely on memory, vet’s reminders, calendar notifications and paper trail.

How might we improve the pet wellness and caretaking experience so that pet owners can be more relaxed, have a more enjoyable time and deeper connection with their pets and family?

Phase 3: Ideation

During the ideation phase, we started off with the I like, I wish, What if method where we organized the information that our interviews gave us which will later help us in creating the key features of our app. We found our key features by creating a Priortization Matrix. Some of them were:

  • Having an interface that allows pet owners to share their pet's information with other caretakers.
  • Digitially storing their pet's records and paperwork.
  • Adding multiple pets and pet profiles.
Value Proposition

"Pal is the only app you need to keep your pet's information in one place."

Phase 5: User Testing and Outcomes
Key Findings
  • There was one too many sign up/sign in screens
  • Users wanted to know where they were in the process of setting up their Pal app and signing up
  • There were too many options to skip tasks that were essential for using Pal
  • Users go through sign up quickly and passively
  • Users wanted to complete another action once they reached the homepage
Iterations Based on User Tests
  • Condensed the sign up sign in screens to one main screen
  • Keyboard for each field in sign up and pet profile - add vertical line to each field when typing so users know they are filling in information
  • Added in Log Activity task on Home Screen for users to complete
  • Add progress bar
  • Adjust skips and back buttons throughout process
  • Keyboards as overlay functionality to make prototype function like real app
